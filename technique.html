The Basic Stances

Here we have the stand by position. This is used
anytime that you are awaiting instructions.
This is the "set" position. This is the position
you have to use for maching.
This is the "carry" position. This is the most common stance for marching without playing. It is also most common for marching with the cadence. The cadence( or percussion cadence) is the term used when either entering/exiting the stadium or marching in a parade.
This is the "briefcase" position. This is a common stance for marching with the cadence.
This is the "swords" position. This is a cadence stance typically used by flutes and clarinets.
